✦ OUR STORY ✦

Colouring The World Of Young People

SambaSports is a youth-centered, non-partisan Organization founded in February 2018 in Kwale County, Kenya. Our mission is to inspire and influence behavioral transformation in adolescents and youth through Sports for Development (S4D).

The S4D model integrates sports, arts, mentorship, life skills training, and value-based education to create transformative opportunities for young people.

We are committed to creating safe spaces for young people, fostering social cohesion, and empowering communities through innovative interventions.
