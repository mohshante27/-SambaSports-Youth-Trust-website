✦ WHO WE ARE ✦

Inspiring Wellness And Influencing Behavioral Transformation in Young People

"We believe every young person deserves the chance to succeed, regardless of their background."

— MOHAMED MWACHAUSA Hsc, Co-Founder & Executive Director

SambaSports Youth Trust uses sports to empower youth, promote education, and build healthier communities in Kwale County and beyond. Through innovative programs and dedicated partnerships, we create lasting change for thousands of young people.
